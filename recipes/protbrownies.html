Yummy (and Healthy) Protein Brownies

Description

Brownies are a staple confection. Growing up, our parents would tell us that brownies were incredibly unhealthy, and when you look at the large calorie count, they were probably right. These brownies are a healthier alternative, that is high in protein, and generally uses healthier ingredients. While you probably shouldn't be eating these every day, they are a much healthier alternative!

Ingredients

Source of Recipe
  • 1 cup almond butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar free maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup Choclate Protein Powder
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup lowfat choclate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease baking pan.
  2. Whisk eggs, almond butter, eggs, maple syrup, almond milk, and vanilla extract
  3. Add protein powder,cocoa poder, sea salt, and baking powder to mixing bowl and continue stirring until combined
  4. Add milk if batter is dry
  5. Fold in choclate chips gently
  6. Now transfer to a baking fan and bake for 25-30 minutes. Let the brownees cool, and cut into squares and enjoy!
